TABLET

The year 2041 and everyone is living happily under the futuristic vibes of the city under futuretech hands, Mr. Fierce, a doctoor determined to make a difference learns that a new virus has been contracted and it is indeed air borne.. The effects? Your body cells decreasing at a very accelerated rate, causing those to feel weak and slowly die. With this, he creates the one pill to save lives in the form of a blue form, with his competition creating a red pill just as powerful. Zion Fierce, the son of the mad scientist has contracted the virus and becomes the first test subject under the hands of his father and contracts extroadinary powers due to the fact that the pill caused his body cells to become greater than ever before. Gaining him super strength, telekenisis and healing. With this, his father becomes obessesed with this side affect and wants to spread it across the galaxy due to the fact that the super powers can save lives, but Zion knows this can cause chaos. Betrayal, Uprising, Hope, Defeat can be found in this non traditional super hero movie of mass proportions, imagine a world where everyone had super powers, would you survive?
